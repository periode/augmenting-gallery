overview

This week, we have our first meeting with the M4P0 team. After learning about their use cases and their visions, we will assemble into groups to work on each of those for the rest of the semester.

Once the groups are assigned, the rest of the session will be dedicated to finishing up the Augmented Exercises in Unity, as well as clarifying any issues that remain with Unity development. It's important to get these out of the way before we move on to more conceptual approaches on the topic of museums and digital media.

m4p0

project

our collaborators started from the Museum4Punkt0 initiative, a federal program aimed at modernizing museums through digital technologies across Germany.

the point of the collaboration is to come up with designs and developments in response to a specific use-case.

the final presentation of your proposal and prototype will be on dec 4th (last day of classes).

people

  • cristina navarro, digital concept and coordination for the staatliche museen, and ethnology museum in the humboldt forum.
  • ceren topcu, communication and concept for the museum für kommunikation, now NEEEU Spaces
  • julia kuhnert, humboldt forum digital team

use-cases

will be uploaded on the class wiki: https://github.com/periode/augmenting-gallery/wiki/M4P0-use-cases

guidelines

the client is having a project, an idea, an issue, and you come up with the solution.

your final presentation should include:

  • a writeup of your concept proposal
    • what you're proposing
    • why you think it matches the client brief
    • how you propose to implement it

and the choice between:

  • 1 technical prototype using AR
    • demonstrating the core features of the app (unity)
  • 2 design prototypes using XD/Figma
    • demonstrating the look and feel of your app (adobe xd)

communication

to prevent information overload, all communication should be done by email, with pierre in cc, and during working hours.
